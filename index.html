Heshie Brody is currently a Technical Lead manager at Morgan and Morgan PA (America's largest personal injury law firm), where he is responsible for guiding the development of client web experiences including hiring, product roadmap and infrastructure. Think about how many thousands of people it takes to run an operation like that and the opportunity for automation, optimization and streamlining a great web experience coupled with AI can bring to a law firm of that size.
Yeah, that's his day job. Pretty cool, isn't?

His extensive background includes a role as a founding engineer at Capable Health, where he contributed significantly to the creation of a scalable API and integration infrastructure.
Prior to this, he worked at Sitepod, focusing on their automated back office product, and at Adar Medical Uniforms, where he developed logistical warehouse solutions incorporating ERP and consumer-facing integrations.
Heshie is an alumnus of the Flatiron School's Software Engineering program.

Originally from Brooklyn, New York, Heshie discovered his passion for technology and tinkering at the age of 9 by disassembling computers. His interest in coding sparked at 14 when he began exploring the developer tab in Excel. He self-taught programming using a "Visual Basic for Dummies" book, which he soon utilized to create a program that simulated a hard drive wipe, humorously installing it on his school's computers.
